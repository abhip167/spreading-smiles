About US

Spreading Smiles

Inspired by Param Pujya Bhaishri Rameshbhai Oza, Spreading Smiles Charitable Trust is an organization working towards making a difference in the field of Education & Health.SS Kids Club is a part of Spreading Smiles which aims at imparting Social & Moral Values to the budding generation.

Focus Areas

Seva hi Jeevan

Inspired by Pujya Bhaishri Rameshbhai Oza, Spreading Smiles Charitable Trust is working towards making a difference in the field of Health, Education & Swachhta. Operating in Vadodara, Gujarat.

Mrs Tripti Pandit & Mr Dhiraj Pandit disciple of P P Bhaishri, founded Spreading Smiles in 2015.

Making a difference to the society is their aim & vision, which manifests in the activities of the Trust.
